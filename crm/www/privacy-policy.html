Privacy Policy

Introduction

At iPelite, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and business data. This Privacy Policy explains how iPelite ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you use our CRM software and related services ("Services"). By using iPelite, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for or use iPelite CRM, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, billing address, and login credentials.
  • Business Data: Customer records, contacts, leads, deals, tasks, notes, communications, and any other CRM data you input into the platform.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details or bank account information (processed securely through third-party payment processors; iPelite does not store raw card data).
  • Profile Information: Job title, company size, industry, and preferences you set within the platform.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access iPelite CRM, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Features used, pages visited, actions performed, and time spent on the platform.
  • Device & Log Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and crash reports.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Session cookies, analytics cookies, and similar technologies to improve user experience. We rely on legitimate interest for analytics cookies and obtain explicit consent for marketing cookies, in accordance with applicable law.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Third-party integrations you authorize (e.g., email clients, accounting software, social media platforms).
  • Business partners, resellers, or referral partners.
  • Publicly available sources for data enrichment purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

iPelite uses collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, operate, maintain, and improve the iPelite CRM platform.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process payments, and send administrative communications.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and deliver relevant features and recommendations.
  • Analytics & Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our software, features, and user interface.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, unauthorized access, and other security issues.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, and other applicable legislation.
  • Marketing: To send promotional communications about iPelite products and features only with your prior consent, where required. You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking 'unsubscribe' in our emails or by contacting us.

3. How We Share Your Information

iPelite does not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party vendors who assist in delivering our services, including cloud hosting providers, payment processors, email delivery services, analytics providers, and customer support tools. These parties are contractually bound to handle data securely, only as directed by us, and may not use your data for their own purposes.

3.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or restructuring, your information may be transferred to the successor entity. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy, and you will have the option to delete your account.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by applicable law or in response to valid legal processes such as court orders or government requests, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of iPelite, our users, or the public. We will notify you of any such disclosure to the extent permitted by law.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with other parties only when you have provided explicit, informed consent to do so.

4. Data Storage and Security

iPelite employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication options.
  • Secure data centers with physical and logical access controls.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely immune to security threats. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required under applicable law, without undue delay.

We encourage users to use strong passwords and report any suspicious activity immediately to privacy@ipelite.com.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with our services. Upon account termination:

  • Active business data is accessible for 30 days, during which you can export your data in a standard machine-readable format.
  • Backup copies are permanently and irreversibly deleted within 90 days of account closure.
  • Certain data may be retained longer if required by applicable legal obligations (e.g., financial records, GST audit logs, court orders).

6. Your Rights and Choices

Under applicable law (including the DPDP Act, 2023), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion (Erasure): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing or profiling purposes at any time.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent was the legal basis, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our designated Grievance Officer or with the Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@ipelite.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request, at no charge to you.

7. Cookies Policy

iPelite uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used under legitimate interest to understand platform usage. You may opt out.
  • Marketing/Preference Cookies: Used only with your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent at any time.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our in-platform cookie preference center. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

8. Children's Privacy

iPelite CRM is a business software solution intended solely for use by individuals who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take immediate steps to delete it and notify the relevant guardian or authority.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in India.

If your data needs to be processed in other countries, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses or other mechanisms recognized under applicable Indian law and the DPDP Act, 2023, to protect your data.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our practices.

We will notify you of any material changes by email or through a prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before the changes take effect.

Your continued use of iPelite after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
